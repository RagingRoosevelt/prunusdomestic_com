Retail Partnership

Hi! If you're responsible for merchandising at a fiber arts or fiber arts-adjacent shop in the Portland metro area and would like to sell one of the designs I make, feel free to drop me an email: partnership@PrunusDomestic.com

Prunus Domestic is a relatively small operation and I'm scaling organically as demand develops. Right now, I'm handling sales on a "consignment" basis. I'll provide inventory and if any sells, you can pay me the wholesale rate for that inventory. I'll take back any inventory you don't want to keep in stock.

If you'd like to arrange a more traditional inventory purchasing situation, I'm happy to discuss that but I'll need to make logistical arrangements on my end to make that happen.

Customizations

I can accommodate customizations if you'd like to place a bulk order for product. Depending on the product, this might mean using graphics or a logo of your choice. Or this could mean an exclusive design that we collaborate on.

Product Development

I'm interested in working with enthusiast fiber arts shops to develop new products. If you'd like help bringing a product to market, I'd love to help you design / engineer it. My focus is on 3D printed products so if it seems like we're looking at an idea that wouldn't be suited to what I can do, I will be upfront about that.
