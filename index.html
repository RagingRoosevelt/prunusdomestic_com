Welcome to Prunus Domestic. I'm a 3D model designer and small scale manufacturer located in Milwaukie, OR, just outside of Portland. I post most of my designs for free on Printables and a few I make and sell locally in the Portland region.

Designs

Sewing Pattern Weights

Use these weights to keep your pattern positioned while marking or cutting out your project!

I'm selling these at Sewcial Studies in downtown Milwaukie.

Turkish Drop Spindle

I wanted to pick up a Jenkin's spindle for my partner but I hadn't been able to find them in stock so I designed my own. It's on Printables if you want to make your own.

Portable Yarn Cake Spinner / Holder

This is handy for keeping your yarn cake from rolling away. You can use the base holder when you're working at a table or desk, or you can set up a lanyard leash and use it from your wrist. It's on Printables if you want to make your own.

8-bay 3U NAS enclosure for 10" Mini-Racks

Just using normal drive shelves, you can get a max of two 3.5" drives per 1U of rack space. This enclosure uses a Jonsbo N3 backplane to enable 2.67 drives per 1U. A small improvement but more is always better when it comes to storage.

I published this design on Printables.
